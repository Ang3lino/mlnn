TensorFlow

Characteristics

  1. A machine learning system that operates at large scale and in heterogeneous environments.
  2. Supports large-scale training and inference.
  3. Uses dataflow graphs to represent computation, shared state, and the operations that mutate that state.
  4. Proposed and extensively used by Google.

M. Abadi et al. Tensorflow: Large-scale machine learning on heterogeneous distributed systems. arXiv preprint arXiv:1603.04467. 2016.

P. Goldsborough A Tour of Tensorflow. arXiv preprint arXiv:1610.01178. 2016.

TensorFlow

Components

  1. Sessions: A special environment for the execution of operations and evaluation of tensors. They also encapsulate the allocation and management of resources.
  2. Operations: Can be a mathematical equation, a variable, a control flow directive, etc. Represented as nodes of the computational graph.
  3. Tensors: A multi-dimensional collection of homogeneous values with a fixed, static type. Represented as edges.
  4. Variables: Persistent, mutable handles to in memory buffers storing tensors.
